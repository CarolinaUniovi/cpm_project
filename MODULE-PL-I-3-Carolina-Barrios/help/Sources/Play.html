Play Ghost Busters

When opening the game, the instructions would be shown. If you need to review them, they are available in the 'Game' section of the menu.

There are two types of discounts you can get:

  • 25%: obtained when hunting one ghost of each of the different 5 types and the leader.
  • 10%: obtained when hunting one ghost of each of the different 5 types.

In any other case, there is no discount.

If you have a discount already saved and you win another one, the former will remain while the other won't.
